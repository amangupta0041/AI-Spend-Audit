# Economics

**Value of a Converted Lead**
If a startup books a consultation and purchases discounted credits through Credex:
- Assume average annual AI spend for a Series A startup is $50,000.
- Credex provides a 20% discount ($10,000 savings) and takes a 10% cut of the original spend as a fee/margin.
- **LTV (1 Year) of a converted lead = $5,000.**

**CAC by Channel**
1. **Cold Outbound (LinkedIn):** $0 in direct spend, but costs time. ~2 hours for 50 DMs -> 2 leads -> $0 CAC (excluding founder time).
2. **Hacker News / Content:** $0 in direct spend. Highly variable, but essentially free organic acquisition.
3. **Founder Slacks:** $0.

**Conversion Math for Profitability**
Let's model a funnel to acquire 10 customers (yielding $50,000 in gross margin/revenue):
- Goal: 10 Credit Purchases
- Assuming a 20% close rate on consultations -> Need 50 Consultations Booked.
- Assuming 15% of captured emails book a call -> Need 333 Emails Captured.
- Assuming 20% of audits result in an email capture -> Need 1,665 Audits Completed.
- Assuming a 25% landing page to audit conversion -> Need 6,660 Unique Visitors.

If we achieve this entirely through organic and low-cost channels, the ROI is massive. Even if we spend $2,000 on targeted sponsorships to drive those 6,660 visitors, the CAC is $200 per converted customer, against an LTV of $5,000. The unit economics are highly profitable.

**Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months**
To reach $1M ARR, assuming the $5k ACV/LTV calculated above, Credex needs **200 closed customers**.
Using our funnel metrics:
- 200 Customers
- 1,000 Consultations Booked
- 6,666 Emails Captured
- 33,333 Audits Completed
- 133,333 Unique Visitors (over 18 months = ~7,400 visitors/month)

**What has to be true:**
1. The tool must organically rank (SEO) for terms like "Cursor vs Copilot pricing" and "Anthropic API cost calculator" to sustain 7.4k monthly visitors without ad spend.
2. The "savings" narrative must remain compelling. If vendors consolidate or slash prices drastically, the delta (and Credex's value prop) shrinks.
3. Credex must maintain inventory of these credits to sell when the lead converts.
