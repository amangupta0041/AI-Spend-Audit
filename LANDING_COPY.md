**FAQ:**
1. **Q: Is this really free?**
   A: Yes. We don't charge for the audit. Our goal is to highlight inefficiencies in the ecosystem.

2. **Q: Do I need to connect my AWS or billing accounts?**
   A: No. Just tell us what tools you use and your estimated spend or seat count. We run the math against current vendor pricing.

3. **Q: How accurate is the savings estimate?**
   A: Very. We pull live pricing data directly from OpenAI, Anthropic, GitHub, and others to calculate exactly what you *should* be paying for your use case.

4. **Q: Why does Credex care about my AI spend?**
   A: Credex helps startups buy discounted AI infrastructure credits. We build tools like this to find founders who have a real need for our core service.

5. **Q: Can I share the report with my CFO?**
   A: Absolutely. Every audit generates a unique, anonymous public URL you can drop in Slack or an email.
